About Us

Engineers Without Borders - Brazil

Engineers Without Borders (EWB) Brazil is a non-profit organization that believes in the power of engineering to transform lives and communities. We are part of the global Engineers Without Borders - International (EWB-I) network and have been operating in Brazil since 2010, positively impacting over 110,000 lives.

Engineers Without Borders - Juiz de Fora Hub

The NGO is present in 65 countries and over 70 Brazilian cities. The Juiz de Fora hub was founded in 2015. Since then, engineering and architecture students and graduates have developed and executed over 70 projects on a completely voluntary basis.

Our hub in Juiz de Fora is composed of student and professional volunteers who believe in engineering and architecture as agents of social transformation.
